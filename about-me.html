About Me

"Healthy families birth healthy societies." In lieu of this, one of my goals is to help build a peaceful society with standard morals. I am a child advocate and life coach; I believe that when a child/teenager is well trained without being deprived of their rights and enlighten them with the necessary education they need for the different stages they experience, a health society is being built.

I teach in Tansol Treasures College at Alaguntan, Iyanapaja, Lagos, Nigeria. I started a club for preteens and teenagers, tagged 'Teenstalk'. I have a friendly and motherly relationship with my students; such that they call me "our school mother" or "the school counselor."

"Having the right mindset plays important role in achieving greatness."

As a lady who is passionate about achieving greatness, I founded 'Mind Reformation Forum' in 2020 with over 200 members currently. It's an online platform where I teach and mentor people, providing them with practical, real life lessons, and principles to live a fulfilled and victorious life on earth.

As a relationship coach, love, and mental therapist, I help address critical issues affecting people's relationships and proffer solutions. I understand the importance of a stable emotion and healthy mentality in productivity.

As an intellectual with eloquent speaking style, I have been invited as guest speaker on different platforms to counsel, motivate and inspire thousands of people who are struggling with difficult situations in their love, marital and personal lives. I write for Edify Network, the monthly article written by her 'Relationship without Regret'.

I currently hold a National Diploma (ND) in Business Administration and Management from Federal Polytechnic, Ede, Osun State.

I am currently studying Psychology at the prestigious University of Ibadan, Oyo state, Nigeria. I inspire and motivate people around me. I am friendly, but also a disciplinarian. I believe that nothing in the real sense is impossible, if one is responsible, accountable and intentional. I just launched my book, Identifying True Love.
