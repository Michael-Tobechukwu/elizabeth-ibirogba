"Healthy families birth healthy societies." In lieu to this, one of her goals is to build a peaceful society with standard morals. She is a child advocate and life coach. She believes that when a child/teenager is well trained without being deprived of their rights and enlighten them with the necessary education they need for the different stages they experience, a health society is being built. Hence; her profession. She teaches in Tansol Treasures College at Alaguntan, Iyanapaja, Lagos State. Nigeria. She established a club for preteens and teenagers, tagged 'teenstalk'. She has a friendly and motherly relationship with the students. Some call her "our school mother" while others call her "the school counselor." "Having the right mindset plays important role in achieving greatness." As a lady who is passionate about achieving greatness together with anyone she come across with, she founded 'Mind Reformation Forum' in 2020 with over 200 members. It's an online platform where she teaches and mentors people, providing them with practical real life lessons, and principles to live a fulfilled and victorious life on earth. Oluwashindara Lizzy Ibirogba is also a relationship coach, love, and mental therapist. She addresses critical issues affecting people's relationships and proffer solutions. She understands the important of a stable emotion and healthy mentality in productivity. As an intellectual with eloquent speaking style, she has been invited as guest speaker on different platforms, on several occasions to counsel, motivate and inspire thousands of people who are passing through difficult situations in their love, marital and personal lives. She writes for Edify Network. The monthly article written by her 'Relationship without Regret' is one of the best monthly articles so far. She holds National Diploma in Business Administration and Management from Federal Polytechnic, Ede, Osun State. She is currently studying Psychology at the prestigious University of Ibadan, Oyo state, Nigeria. Within and outside work, she remains the same. Her personality inspires and motivates people around her. She is friendly but also a disciplinarian. Her resilience is worthy of emulation. She believes that nothing in the real sense is impossible, if one is responsible, accountable and intentional. Her lifestyle and conduct spells that out. She is the author of one of the best selling books; Identifying True Love.
