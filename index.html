About Me

Hello, my name is Oluwashindara Elizabeth Iborogba, but you can call me Lizzy. Welcome to my website.
I believe healthy families birth healthy societies and that's why I started the 'Mind Reformation Forum' in 2020 which currently has over 200 members.
As a relationship coach, love and mental therapist, I help people address critical issues affecting their lives and relationships; helping them gain mastery of their emotions and thus, be more productive. I am currently studying Psychology at the University of Ibadan (UI), Oyo state, Nigeria.
